Library Rules & Regulations

Please follow these guidelines to maintain a peaceful learning environment.

General Guidelines

Membership

  • Only registered members are allowed to borrow books.
  • Members must carry their valid Library ID card while entering the library.
  • Loss of ID card must be reported immediately to the librarian.

Borrowing & Returning

  • Students can borrow up to 2 books at a time.
  • Books must be returned within 14 days from the date of issue.
  • Late return fines: ₹5 per day will be charged for overdue books.
  • Books can be renewed only if there is no demand from other members.

Behavior in Library

  • Maintain complete silence inside the library premises.
  • Mobile phones must be kept on silent mode or vibration mode.
  • Eating, drinking, or chewing gum is strictly prohibited.
  • Do not rearrange books on the shelves; leave them on the table after reading.

Care of Library Materials

  • Handle all books and journals with care.
  • Any damage or loss of library material must be replaced or paid for by the member.
  • Writing, underlining, or tearing pages from books is strictly forbidden.

⚠️ Important Notice

Violation of any of the above rules may lead to suspension of library privileges. For any dispute, the decision of the Librarian will be final.
